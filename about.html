Our Story

Koki Restaurant was founded in 2020 with a passion for serving fresh, flavorful Kenyan and fusion dishes. What started as a small kitchen has grown into a cozy spot loved for its warm service and delicious meals.
From classic favorites like pilau, nyama choma, and fresh juices, to modern twists on local dishes, Koki is where good food and good vibes meet. Whether you're dining in or taking away, you're always welcome here.
Karibu Koki — Taste the difference.



Our Mission

At Koki Restaurant, our mission is to serve fresh, authentic, and affordable meals that celebrate Kenyan flavors and bring people together.
We’re committed to quality, consistency, and warm hospitality, creating a dining experience that feels like home — every time.



Meet our Team

Behind every delicious dish at Koki Restaurant is a passionate team dedicated to quality, service, and hospitality.

  • Chef David – Head Chef With over 10 years of culinary experience, Chef David brings bold flavors and creative twists to our traditional recipes. His love for local ingredients is at the heart of every meal.
  • Susan – Restaurant Manager Susan ensures that every guest feels welcomed and at home. Her attention to detail and warm personality keep things running smoothly on the floor.

  • Kitchen & Service Team Our hardworking kitchen staff and friendly wait team make sure your experience is memorable — from prep to plate.
